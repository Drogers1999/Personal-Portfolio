  • About Me

    Hi, I’m Daniel Rogers, a Full Stack Developer based in Manchester, UK. With six months of hands-on experience building web applications, I am committed to continuous learning and refining my skills to deliver impactful and innovative solutions.

    My journey into development began with a deep curiosity about how web and software applications are created. Seeing lines of code in movies or from a friend’s screen—abstract and complex—sparked my fascination and eventually led me to write my first line of code.

    Since then, I’ve developed a passion for building, discovering, and implementing new techniques, as well as solving challenging problems. Every project reinforces my love for development, and I am excited to continue growing as a developer while contributing to meaningful and creative solutions.
